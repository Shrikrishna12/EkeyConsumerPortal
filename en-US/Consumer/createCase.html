Privacy Policy

In order to help resolve the reported issue, any information that has been provided as part of the Registration (“Registration Information”) may be shared with our call centre, consumer affairs team, internal departments of the TRA and your service provider, in accordance with our Registration Privacy Policy. By checking this box, you are accepting and consenting to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

Registration Privacy Policy – Registration Information
Telecommunication Regulatory Authority (“We”) are committed to protecting and respecting your privacy. This policy sets out the basis on which any personal data we collect from you, or that you provide to us, when registering a complaint, enquiry and/or a suggestion, will be processed by us. Please read the following carefully to understand our views and practices regarding your personal data and how we will treat it.

Information We May Collect From You
We may collect and process the following information about you:

  • Information you give us: this includes information you provide when you register to submit a suggestion, submit an enquiry and/or submit a complaint]. The information you give us may include your name, address, e-mail address, phone number, financial and credit card information and any other information in an e-mail that related to a complaint, suggestions and or enquiry (the “Registration Information”.

Information Sharing

  • In order to effectively resolve any issues that you have raised, your Registration Information may be shared with our call centre, consumer affairs team, internal departments of the TRA and your service provider.
  • The table below sets out the particular groups which may have sight of your Registration Information, and the reason why your Registration Information may be shared with each of them: Your Registration Information may be shared with: The purpose of sharing this information is: TRA Call Centre To validate the information you have submitted on the portal and in any supporting documents you have provided. This will allow a case to be generated, followed up and closed once your issue has been resolved.
TRA Internal Directorates To review your Registration Information and supporting documents. The information provided will allow action points for your Service Provider to be raised
Your Service Provider To review your Registration Information and supporting documents in order to resolve your issue.

Changes to this Registration Privacy Policy

  • Any changes we may make to our Registration Privacy Policy in future will be posted on this page, and where appropriate, notified to you by e-mail.

Contact
Questions, comments and requests regarding this Privacy Policy should be addressed to:

  • Consumer Affairs
    Telecommunications Regulatory Authority
    P. O. Box 10353
